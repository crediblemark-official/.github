## Analysis Before Development

A good system does not start with a feature list. Workflows, user needs, bottlenecks, and business goals must be understood first. Once the problem is clear, we determine if the solution is a process refinement, website, custom app, integration, automation, or using existing tools. This approach ensures every digital solution is built on a solid foundation — not assumptions.

## What You Get

Crediblemark gives you analysis before development, so problems and needs are mapped before any features or technologies are decided. You communicate directly with the founder and developer working on your project — no middlemen, no communication gaps. The project scope is transparent from the start: features, costs, duration, and deliverables are all agreed before development begins. Progress is trackable through a project dashboard. ## Consult · Design · Build

Three integrated phases connect analysis, development, and post-launch support into one unified workflow.

**Consult** — We analyze problems, workflows, user needs, risks, and business goals. The result becomes your project blueprint and scope.

**Design** — System architecture, data flows, UI/UX, and technical specifications are designed based on the agreed blueprint.

**Build** — Websites, applications, databases, dashboards, and integrations are built and launched according to the blueprint, with transparent and trackable progress.

## Solutions Built on Business Needs

Crediblemark does not use a one-size-fits-all approach. Each system is designed based on workflows, users, and business goals.

**Growth System — Websites for Sales and Credibility.** Company profile websites, landing pages, product catalogs, and business sites that help build trust and generate leads. When your online presence needs to do more than just exist — it needs to convert.

**Operation System — Applications and Operational Systems.** CRM, inventory, booking systems, dashboards, customer portals, and admin systems that reduce manual work and improve data accuracy. For businesses drowning in spreadsheets, scattered chat messages, and repeated data entry.

**Support System — Integration and System Support.** API integration, deployment, backups, maintenance, and further development to keep systems stable and growing with your business needs. Because building the system is only half the journey — keeping it running and evolving is what matters long term.
